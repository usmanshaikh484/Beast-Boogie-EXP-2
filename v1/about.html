From Houston Gyms to Global Stages

Anthony "Beast Boogie" Pepper is a legendary B-Boy who has dedicated his life to the craft of breaking and hip-hop culture. With 28 years of experience, he has performed on the world's biggest stages, from NBA All-Star games to international dance festivals.

Beyond the performances, Beast Boogie is a passionate educator committed to using dance as a tool for empowerment. He has touched the lives of over 100,000 students through his dynamic school assemblies and workshops, inspiring young people to find confidence, community, and creativity through breaking.

His mission is clear: bring the energy, culture, and positive message of hip-hop and breaking to audiences everywhere, one performance and one student at a time.
